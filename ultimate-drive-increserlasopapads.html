Articulating Cab

The Cabra's paramount feature is its patented articulating driver and passenger cab. The cab raises hydraulically and can be driven in this state for a superior view of terrain and game. The cab can be lowered on the fly to reduce the center of gravity. There are only 3 steps to enter the cab instead of a ladder or flight of stairs.

Hybrid electric

The Cabra utilizes a lithium ion battery pack to drive electric motors on both the front and rear axles for silent four-wheel-drive operation for approximately 35 miles. There is also a built-in gas powered generator they can extend your range to over 160 miles-so you never have to worry about running low on battery far from the Lodge.

Clean sheet design

The Cabra isn’t just another truck or SUV with a platform and steering wheel stuck on top. It was designed from the ground up to be the greatest brush country hunting rig ever developed. The all aluminum chassis was laid out to make the best use of space for dog boxes, gun storage, feeders etc.

Convertible top & folding windshield

In just a couple of minutes, you can raise or collapse the convertible top for optimal shade. Bring up the foldable windshield to protect those in the cab from unnecessary dust and debris while using the built-in windshield wipers to keep your vision clear.

Dog boxes
