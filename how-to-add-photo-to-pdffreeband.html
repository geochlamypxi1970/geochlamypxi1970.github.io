How to Convert Picture to PDF on iPhone and iPad

  1. Open the Photos app on your iPhone and tap on Albums. Now, tap on Select and choose one or multiple images.

How To Add Pdfs Together

  1. Open the Files app and locate the image.
  2. To convert a single image into PDF: Long press on the image and choose Create PDF.
  3. To convert several images into PDF: Tap on the ellipsis icon (three dots inside a circle) from the top right and tap on Select. Now, tap on multiple photos. Once done, tap on the ellipsis icon from the bottom right and choose Create PDF.

The Files app will make a PDF out of those images. It will get saved in the same location as the original pictures. You can see our full guide on using the Files app to copy, move, or share this PDF.
