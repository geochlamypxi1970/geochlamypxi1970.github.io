Below you’ll find more about the markets we offer to help you understand what you’ll be placing your bets on:
  • To Win: This is a bet on a horse to win the race. If the horse finishes in any position other than first, then the bet loses.
  • Each Way: This is a bet on a horse to either win or place in a race. This requires you to double your stake as it is essentially two separate bets. One part of the bet is for the horse to win the race, with the second part being on the horse to place (usually in the top 3/4). If your horse wins the race, both aspects of the bet will pay out. If the horse only places, you will lose the To Win section of the bet, but win the part of the bet which is on a horse to place. Of course, if the horse finishes outside the places, your entire bet loses.
