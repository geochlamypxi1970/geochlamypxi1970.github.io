Some FAQs

How to temporarily pause an antivirus?

It is very important to temporarily disable the antivirus program while using any of the specialized tools to prevent and cross-interaction.

To do that, go to the bottom right of the system tray until you locate the small icon of your antivirus software. Right click on the icon and select the preferred disable duration from 10 minutes, 1 hour, until the system restart, or permanently.

How to uninstall an antivirus?

Uninstalling an antivirus program does not cancel the subscription. So it is better to temporarily disable it if you are facing a minor issue.

To uninstall an antivirus, click on the Windows Start menu and find your antivirus program in the list. Open the program and look for an uninstall option. From there it is an auto-uninstallation process, you just have to follow the prompts until to an end.

How to disable antivirus?

It is necessary to disable an antivirus while installing some new program into the system. Because often antivirus software fails to distinguish between a legitimate program and a virus settling in the computer’s storage system.

In order to do that, go to the notification area present on the lower right of the taskbar and right-click on an antivirus icon, then select the option to either disable or exit the program.
